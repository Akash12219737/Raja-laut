When to come

Best Time to Visit

Bunaken offers exceptional diving year-round thanks to its tropical climate and consistently warm water temperatures (27-29°C / 81-84°F).

However, the dry season from April to October generally offers the best visibility, reaching up to 30 meters or more. The sea is often calmer during these months, making for comfortable boat crossings.

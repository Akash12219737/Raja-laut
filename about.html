About Us

The Raja Laut Philosophy

Raja Laut Dive Resort is the perfect combination for guests who love diving with a professional support and at the same time enjoy a relaxing stay in a friendly and premium context.

We are dedicated to sustainable eco-tourism, ensuring that our beautiful Bunaken Marine Park is protected for generations to come. Our custom-built eco bungalows preserve the natural beauty without sacrificing luxury.
